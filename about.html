South Carolina

Hurricanes & Tropical Storms

About

This application lets you search for and view information for over 300 years of hurricanes and tropical storms that have impacted South Carolina. You will find storm track data for all storms after 1851, when official records began. In addition, information and comments specific to South Carolina are included, which make this a very rich dataset for the State of South Carolina and beyond.

Get Data

The historical track data in this application were compiled from HURDAT2 and modified by the South Carolina State Climate Office for use with this application. Additionally, the State Climate Office compiled information for storms prior to 1851. No storm track data are available for these storms, but information about their impacts to South Carolina can be found in the application.

The GIS web services used in this application are publicly available and can be viewed on ArcGIS Online.

Explanations of Data

Storm Details

Storm Name

Official Storm Name from HURDAT2

Storm Year

Year the storm occured.

Max Category

Maximum status and category that was reached at any time during the storm's duration. Storm status was obtained from HURDAT2. Category was given if the storm reached hurricane status, and the category was calculated from the wind speeds given in HURDAT2 using the Saffir-Simpson hurricane wind scale.

Max Wind

Maximum wind recorded during the storm's duration, obtained from HURDAT2. Units are in knots in HURDAT2, but converted to miles per hour (mph) for the web application.

Min Pressure

Mininum pressure recorded during the storm's duration, obtained from HURDAT2. Units are in millibars.

Landfall Locations

Landfall locations were obtained from a variety of sources compiled by South Carolina State Climate Office staff.

SC Impacts Dates

Dates when the storm is estimated to have impacted the State of South Carolina. The South Carolina State Climate Office compiled this information over the years from a variety of sources.

SC Impacts Category

Storm status and category as the storm impacted the State of South Carolina. There are many variables that influence the impacts of a tropical storm or hurricane on any one place.

Comments

Comments are provided here that give a brief summary of the storm's impact to South Carolina.
